30 Finsbury Square is designed to be a wellbeing-focused, sustainable space where people, businesses and nature all have what they need to flourish. The newly updated interior of the building will offer a distinctly different look and feel while retaining 90% of the original structure, helping to reduce environmental impact, minimise energy and materials.
